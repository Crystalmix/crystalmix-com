Our services Crystalnix has built and launched 50+ cloud and mobile apps for companies from startups to enterprises.
Product Development Strategy Strategy & Roadmap Before building anything every member of a team must clearly realize the requirements which always saves time & budget. Depending on how detailed the customer’s Request For Proposal (RFP) is we allocate a zero phase to outline and understand all the requirements for the first version of the app.
Requirements A business analyst works with the customer conducting meetings and discussing the requirements. The outcome is a detailed document with requirements for the first release.
Quotes A team lead is assigned to a project. His main responsibility is to give quotes for time & Materials according to the RFP for inclusion in the Statement of Work.
Wireframes A design lead outlines the app concept by creating its UX wireframes to show the understanding of requirements and provide a basic structure for the project’s design.
Statement of Work Everything is then collected in a single statement of work along with the estimation and cost. According to our experience, it's better to start an iteration when the corresponding UI is ready.
Post Release Maintenance App Care
What next? This is what happens next to the app’s release. A user base grows, new version of mobile platforms are released, new competitors are listed in a store and new feature requests are appeared.
Updates & support A successful app that brings value is always in need of continuous updates. Apart from the implementation of new features it typically requires: ASO (App store optimization), behaviour analytics, users’ support and native OS upgrades.
